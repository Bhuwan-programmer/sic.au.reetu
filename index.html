About SIC


Skyline International College is an Australian Registered Training Organisation operating under the National Vocational Education and Training (VET) Quality Framework located in the heart of Sydney city in NSW Australia. This is a regulated framework which is administered by the Australian Skills Quality Authority (ASQA) and Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students. Skyline is an approved organisation to offer services to international student. Skyline is regulated by ASQA under the Education Services for Overseas Students.
